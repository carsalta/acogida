Privacy Notice – Safety Induction

Controller: DANONE S.A. (Site ESDA242).

Purpose: manage the safety induction for VISITORS and CONTRACTORS, record video/test completion, issue the certificate, and verify its validity.

Legal basis: compliance with health and safety obligations, and DANONE’s legitimate interest in controlling access and evidencing preventive training.

Data processed: full name, ID document, company, email, site, type (visitor/contractor), certificate ID, issuance and expiry dates.

Retention: until the certificate expiry date (12 months contractor; 24 months visitor) plus one additional month for administrative closure; thereafter the record is deleted.

Recipients: no sharing except legal obligations; Google Workspace (Sheets) is used as processor for the registry.

Rights: you can exercise access, rectification, erasure, objection and restriction by emailing cert@danone.com or via Danone’s privacy channel.

More information: please refer to Danone’s Personal Data Privacy Policy.
